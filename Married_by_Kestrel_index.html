About Me

A little more about me! I hope that you feel safe and supported by me and think it helps to get to know me a bit! I grew up in the Black Hills of South Dakota and believe that people, relationships, and experiences are at the center of a fulfilled life. I've admired the art of capturing those moments and stories whether through journalism, podcasts, photography, or sharing reflections and memories with friends. Years ago while I was still in South Dakota, I thought about launching an officiant business; life got busy, but in 2026 I had the honor of officiating a couple of my friends' weddings and was reinspired to start Married by Kestrel! I attended law school at the University of New Hampshire in Concord, NH and enjoy hiking, pickleball, and trying new recpies! I love hosting small dinners with friends, new experiences, attending local events, and soaking up the beauty of daily life on the seacoast.

Ceremonies & Pricing

Every ceremony I officiate is thoughtfully written to reflect your relationship, values, and vision for your wedding day. Whether you're planning an intimate elopement or celebrating with family and friends, I'll work with you to create a ceremony that feels authentic and uniquely yours. Before you commit (pun intended), I am happy to provide a free 15 minute Teams meeting to chat, get to know each other, and discuss a bit about your ceremony vision. If you decide to have me officiate, we would exhange some paperwork and then collaborate on a drafted ceremony script to revise to ensure it's perfect for your day. For wedding booking, it's best to reach out to me with your date and time and I'll confirm my availability.

If your venue is further than 25 miles from Portsmouth, NH, there will be the additional cost at the current state rate per mile to and from the venue. For 2026, it is 72.5 cents per mile.
