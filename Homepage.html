Married by Kestrel

Hi, I'm Hannah Kestrel, and I'm so happy you're here! I'm a wedding officiant based in the New Hampshire seacoast, serving all of New England. Your ceremony is the heart of your special day and should be genuine, sincere, and a reflection of your relationship and commitment. My role is to help you share your story and enter your marriage in the way you want! I've been practicing storytelling and speaking since high school with training in debate, persuasive speech, and as client-focused attorney. You are the stars of your day and I will ensure your love and commitments to each other are felt by all. I firmly stand in support of human rights, queer love, and gay marriage - it would be my honor to marry you, whoever you are!!

About Me

A little more about me! I hope that you feel safe and supported by me and think it helps to get to know me a bit! I grew up in the Black Hills of South Dakota and believe that people, relationships, and experiences are at the center of a fulfilled life. I've admired the art of capturing those moments and stories whether thorugh journalism, podcasts, photography, or sharing reflections and memories with friends. Years ago while I was still in South Dakota, I thought about launching an officiant business; life got busy, but in 2026 I had the honor of officiating a couple of my friends' weddings and was reinspired to start Married by Kestrel! I attended law school at the University of New Hampshire in Concord, NH and enjoy hiking, pickleball, and trying new recpies! I love hosting small dinners with friends, new expereinces, attending local events, and soaking up the beauty of daily life on the seacoast.

Ceremonies & Pricing

Before you commit (pun intended), I am happy to provide a free 15 minute Teams meeting to chat, get to know each other, and discuss a bit about your ceremony vision. If you decide to have me officiate, we would exhange some paperwork and then collaborate on a drafted ceremony script to revise to ensure it's perfect for your day. For wedding booking, it's best to reach out to me with your date and time and I'll confirm my availability. If your venue is further than 25 miles from Portsmouth, NH, there will be the additional cost at the current state rate per mile to and from the venue. For 2026, it is 72.5 cents per mile.

Short & Sweet

Short, intimate ceremonies. Perfect for elopements or those looking for a short and sweet ceremony without too much talking, readings, or vows. Include drafting and rivising the script, a 15 minute call, and up to one hour attendance for the ceremony.

Starting at $150

Wedding Ceremonies

For the more traditional wedding ceremony. Perfect for those desiring a fuller ceremony sharing their love story, a reading, and personal vows. Includes drafting and revising the ceremony script, a 30 minute call, and up to an hour and a half ceremony attendance.

Starting at $300
