Let's Get You Married!

Hi, I'm Hannah Kestrel! I'm a wedding officiant based out of the New Hampshire seacoast, serving the New England region.

Your ceremony should feel like the most genuine part of your wedding day. It's the time that is exclusively about sharing your story, your relationship, and your commitment to each other. I've been practicing storytelling since high school and have extensive public speaking training thorugh debate, leadership, and law school. My role is to ensure your story is told in a way that reflects the people you are and the life you're building together!

I unquestionably support queer love, and proivde services that are rooted in your relationship and general spirituality. I am not a good fit if you'd like religion in your ceremony.


Whether you're planning an intimate elopement, a mountaintop adventure, or a celebration surrounded by everyone you love, I will help create a ceremony that reflects who you are together.
